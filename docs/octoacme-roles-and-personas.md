# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge technical and business stakeholders, ensuring clarity of requirements and alignment between business objectives and technical implementation. They gather, document, and validate requirements throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business and technical requirements
- Bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams
- Clarify project scope and identify ambiguities
- Participate in requirements validation and acceptance testing
- Maintain traceability between business needs and delivered features
- Identify process improvements and suggest optimizations

### Goals
- Ensure accurate translation of business needs into technical solutions
- Reduce rework and scope creep through clear requirements
- Improve team alignment on project objectives and success criteria
- Facilitate effective communication across functional teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders
- Design reviews and specification documents
- Collaboration with Product Managers, Developers, and QA
- Acceptance criteria validation and sign-off

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user research and design efforts to ensure that deliverables are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs. They advocate for the user experience throughout the product development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Design wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Establish and enforce usability and accessibility standards
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and testing results
- Document design decisions and create design systems or style guides
- Ensure WCAG compliance and accessibility best practices

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support burden through better design
- Align product design with business and user goals
- Maintain consistency across user touchpoints

### Typical Communication
- User research findings and insights
- Design specifications and design system documentation
- Collaboration with Product Managers, Developers, and Business Analysts
- Accessibility and usability reviews

---

## Support Leads

### Role Summary
Support Leads coordinate post-release activities, manage customer-facing issues, and ensure that production problems are triaged and escalated appropriately. They serve as a critical feedback loop from customers to the development team.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate post-release support and monitoring
- Triage and prioritize production issues
- Escalate critical issues to the development team
- Gather and relay customer feedback for product improvements
- Document known issues and workarounds
- Participate in incident response and post-mortem reviews
- Track support metrics and identify recurring issues

### Goals
- Minimize customer impact from production issues
- Provide rapid response and resolution to critical problems
- Capture actionable customer insights for product refinement
- Improve system reliability and user satisfaction

### Typical Communication
- Incident alerts and escalation notices
- Customer feedback and support tickets
- Post-incident retrospectives and action items
- Status updates to Project Managers and stakeholders
